## How to Update in Facebook Ads Manager

1. Go to [ads.facebook.com](https://ads.facebook.com)
2. Navigate to **Ads** tab
3. Find each ad listed above
4. Click on the ad name to edit
5. In the **Destination** section, replace the current URL with the corresponding UTM link from above
6. Save changes

## After Update

Once these URLs are updated, all new Facebook ad traffic will be tracked with:
- **Source:** `facebook-ads` (instead of just `facebook`)
- **Medium:** `paid-social` 
- **Campaign:** `capital-wealth-federal-seminar-event`
- **Content:** Specific ad name
- **Term:** Ad set targeting

This will clearly distinguish paid Facebook traffic from organic Facebook referrals in your lead reports! 🎯
